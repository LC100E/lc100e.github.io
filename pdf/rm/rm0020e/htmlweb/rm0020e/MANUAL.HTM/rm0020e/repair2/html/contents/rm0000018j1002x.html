1. INSTALL TRANSMITTER BATTERY



  1. Install a new battery (lithium battery) with the positive (+) side up as shown in the illustration.

    NOTICE:
    1. Be sure that the positive side and the negative side of the transmitter battery are matched correctly.
    2. Be careful not to bend the terminals of the transmitter battery during installation.
    3. Be careful that dust and oil do not come into contact with the transmitter case.

  1. Install the transmitter cover.
