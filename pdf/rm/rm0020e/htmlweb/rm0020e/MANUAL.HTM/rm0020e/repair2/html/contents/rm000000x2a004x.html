RADIATOR GRILLE > REMOVAL

for Preparation Click here



1. REMOVE RADIATOR GRILLE



  1. Remove the 3 screws and grille.



2. REMOVE RADIATOR GRILLE EMBLEM



  1. Put protective tape around the emblem.

  1. Insert a piano wire between the grille and emblem.

  1. Tie objects that can serve as handles (for example, wooden blocks) to both wire ends.

  1. Pull the piano wire and scrape off the double-sided tape that holds the emblem to the grille.

    HINT:
    1. If reusing the emblem, take care not to damage the emblem.
    2. Be careful not to damage the grille.

  1. Remove the emblem.
