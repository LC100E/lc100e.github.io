HOW TO TROUBLESHOOT ECU CONTROLLED SYSTEMS > GENERAL INFORMATION


  1. A large number of ECU controlled systems are used in the LAND CRUISER. In general, ECU controlled systems are considered to be very intricate, requiring a high level of technical knowledge to troubleshoot. However, most problem checking procedures only involve inspecting the ECU controlled system's circuits one by one. An adequate understanding of the system and a basic knowledge of electricity is enough to perform effective troubleshooting, accurate diagnoses and necessary repairs.
  1. FOR USING INTELLIGENT TESTER
    1. Connect the cable of the intelligent tester (with CAN VIM) to the DLC3, turn the ignition switch ON and attempt to use the tester. If the display indicates that a communication error has occurred, there is a problem either with the vehicle or with the tester.
      1. If communication is normal when the tester is connected to another vehicle, inspect the DLC3 of the original vehicle.
      1. If communication is still not possible when the tester is connected to another vehicle, the problem may be in the tester itself. Consult the Service Department listed in the tester's instruction manual.
