RADIATOR GRILLE > INSTALLATION

for Preparation Click here


HINT:
When installing the emblem, heat the grille and emblem using a heat light.

Standard heating temperature:
Item
Temperature
Grille, Emblem
20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:
Do not heat the grille and emblem excessively.



1. INSTALL RADIATOR GRILLE EMBLEM

  1. Clean the grille surface.

    1. Using a heat light, heat the grille surface.

    2. Remove the double-sided tape from the grille.

    3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

  1. If reusing the emblem:
    Clean the emblem.

    1. Using a heat light, heat the emblem.

    2. Remove the double-sided tape from the emblem.

    3. Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

    4. Apply new double-sided tape to the emblem.



  1. Install the emblem.

    1. Using a heat light, heat the grille and emblem.

    2. Remove the peeling paper from the face of the emblem.

      HINT:
      After removing the peeling paper, keep the exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.




    3. Install the emblem.



2. INSTALL RADIATOR GRILLE



  1. Install the grille with the 3 screws.
